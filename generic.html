History of Cancer

A brief History of Cancer

The earliest found refrences to Cancer were found in Egyptian Mummies. They found fossilized bone tumors on the Human Mummies. There were also refrences to cancer on Ancient Manuscripts. The person who dubbed this disease Cancer(he called it the greek word for crab) was the Greek Physician Hippocrates (Hippocrates is known as the Father of Medicine). The name was translated in to Latin and became known as Cancer.

During the 15th to the 18th Centuries. The Human Race's understanding of our body was developing. Doctors in this time were using autopsies to discover the root of diseases and to find out how the human body works. This created the foundation of what we undestand about cancer.
